About Us

We are a blog that seeks to bring to light the recent issues pertaining to the Great Barrier Reef, a chain of coral reefs just outside of Australia.

What's happening?

Due to a plethora of ongoing events in the world, the Great Barrier Reef is a large risk of dying. Such events include global warming, an increased demand for coal mining, and harmful chemicals running into the ocean as a result of industrial processes.
